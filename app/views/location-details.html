getting to the conference centre

Where it is located

Affiliate World Asia takes place from 7-9 December 2015 at the Centara Grand and Bangkok Convention Centre at CentralWorld in Bangkok, Thailand. A small number of events will take place at locations off site which are yet to be confirmed. This information will be updated as soon as it is available.

general information

Registration

You will need to collect your registration pass to be admitted to all Affiliate World Asia events. The registration desk will be open from Sunday, 6 December (time TBC) at the 6th floor of the Centara Grand.
