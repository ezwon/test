Where it is located

A small number of events will take place at locations off site which are yet to be confirmed. This information will be updated as soon as it is available.

accommodation and visitor information

Accommodation

For your convenience, discounted rates have been negotiated at the Centara Grand hotel, the convention centre’s hotel. Share our Facebook message and we will send you the link to access the discount code.

Airports

Bangkok has two international airports which are both approximately 25 km from the Centara Grand Convention Centre, Suvarnabhumi International Airport (BKK) and Don Mueang International Airport (DMK).

Public Transport

For all press related inquiries, please contact us at Affiliate World Conferences.

International Attendees

We recommend that you check with your local government for information on required documents and arrangements you’ll need for travel to Thailand. We are unable to provide advice on your personal requirements. If you need a letter to accompany a visa application to confirm your attendance to Affiliate World Asia, you can contact us.

Bangkok Visitor Information

Visit the Bangkok Tourism Division website to learn about the sights, shops, restaurants, and Thai culture you can discover while you are in the city.

in case of emergencies

Medical and Other Emergencies

For emergency services, (Fire, Medical, Police), please dial the respective numbers from any internal phone or find any uniformed security guard.
Fire: 199 Medical: 1669 Police: 191

Earthquakes

Should an earthquake occur, remain calm and take cover under a stable object. Face away from glass, unstable objects, and tall structures. Remain where you are until the shaking stops and wait for further instructions from authorities. If directed to evacuate the building, walk to the nearest exit. Do not use the elevators and do not return to the building until authorities indicate that it is safe to do so.

Fire

In case of fire, leave the building immediately by walking to the nearest safe exit. Leave all belongings. Do not use the elevators. Do not return to the building until authorities indicate that it is safe to do so.

Non-emergency Medical Assistance

For general non-emergency medical assistance, please approach one of the Affiliate World Conferences uniformed staff members or the Information Desk located on the 22nd floor of the Centara Grand and Bangkok Convention Centre.

attendance terms and conditions

A. Ticket Payments and Delivery

  1. In consideration of the Ticket price (as applicable and as advertised on our website), we shall grant the Purchaser a Ticket to the Conference, subject to these Terms and Conditions. Purchase of a Ticket and/or entry to the Conference shall be deemed to be acceptance of these Terms and Conditions.
  2. We reserve the right to refuse entry to any Attendee who cannot produce evidence of a valid, fully-paid Ticket.
  3. All Tickets must be paid for in full prior to entry to the Conference.
  4. Following payment, Tickets shall be delivered in electronic soft-copy via email to the email address and bearing the Attendee’s name as nominated by the Purchaser. The Ticket acts as a receipt for the transaction and can be used to redeem entry at the Conference. A printed hard-copy Ticket or an electronic soft-copy Ticket detailing the reference number can be used to register at the Conference.
  5. The Ticket must be used by the individual named on the Ticket. Attendees must produce valid identification on request.

B. Conduct at the Conference

Attendees entering the Conference with an Affiliate Ticket may not promote, advertise or represent any company or entity active in the affiliate marketing industry, including but not limited to by wearing branded clothing.

Clause 2.1 shall also apply to Attendees entering the Conference with a Company Ticket only when attending a traffic-focussed networking event.

We may refuse entry to any Attendee who we believe, at our discretion, may have a detrimental effect on the success of the Conference, or the well-being of other Attendees.
